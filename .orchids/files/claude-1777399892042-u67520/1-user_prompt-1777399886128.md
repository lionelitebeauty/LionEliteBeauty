First: What “Start Program” SHOULD DO

This is NOT a “buy now” action.

👉 It should lead to:
A short qualification + onboarding form

Goal:

Filter serious clients
Understand their needs
Position you as premium
🔥 The Strategy (important)

Don’t think:

“Collect info”

Think:

“Pre-frame the client + increase perceived value before they even talk to me”

🧠 Where the button should go
Menu:

👉 “Start Program” → goes to:
Application / Intake Page

Program cards:

👉 “Apply / Get Started” → goes to:
Same form, but tagged to that specific program

🔥 The Form (THIS is what matters)

You don’t want a boring intake form.

You want something that:

Builds authority
Makes them feel selected
Qualifies them
🧾 Form Structure (High-Converting Version)
1. Intro Section (VERY IMPORTANT)

Before the form even starts:

Headline:
“Apply for a Personalized Peptide Optimization Program”

Subtext:
“This short application helps us understand your goals and determine the best protocol for you.”

👉 Optional (powerful):
“We only take on a limited number of clients at a time.”

2. Basic Info (keep it light)
Full Name
Email
Phone (optional but recommended)
3. Primary Goal (this is key)

👉 Multiple choice:

“What is your main goal?”

Skin improvement / anti-aging
Fat loss
Muscle recovery / performance
Hormonal optimization
General wellness
Other: ______
4. Current Situation

“Where are you currently at?”

Beginner (new to peptides)
Some experience
Advanced / previously used protocols
5. Pain Points / Problems

👉 This is where they sell themselves to you

“What are you currently struggling with?”

(Open text)

6. Timeline / Urgency

“How soon are you looking to start?”

ASAP
Within 2–4 weeks
Just exploring
7. Budget Framing (VERY important for high-ticket)

You don’t need exact numbers—just filter seriousness.

“What level of investment are you comfortable with for a personalized program?”

$100–300/month
$300–700/month
$700+/month

👉 This alone filters out 80% of low-quality leads

8. Commitment Level

“How committed are you to following a structured protocol?”

Very committed
Somewhat committed
Just exploring
9. Optional: Health Background (light version)

Keep it simple for now:

“Any relevant health conditions or concerns?”

10. CTA

👉 Not “Submit”

Use:

“Submit Application”
“Apply for Program”
“Start My Optimization Plan”
🔥 After They Submit (CRITICAL)

Don’t leave them hanging.

Redirect Page:

“Application Received”

Then:

👉 “We’ll review your responses and reach out within 24–48 hours”

Optional:
👉 Link to book a call (Calendly)

This is where you can insert your link:
https://calendly.com/a-ringfield-trustetc

🧠 Advanced Move (highly recommended)

If they’re HIGH intent:

👉 After form → show:
“Book Your Consultation Call”

This speeds up conversion massively.

⚠️ Mistakes to Avoid
❌ Too long form (people drop off)
❌ Too medical (feels intimidating)
❌ Too vague (no filtering)
❌ Asking EVERYTHING upfront
